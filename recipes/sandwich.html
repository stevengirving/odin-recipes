Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

A classic staple of many households, the easy peanut butter sandwich goes far and is versatile for any quick snack

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter

Directions

  1. Place two slices of bread on plate
  2. Using a butter knife, spread peanut butter over your slices
  3. Squish pieces together, peanut butter facing inward, and voila!
