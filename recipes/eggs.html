Instant Pot Sous Vide Eggs

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 4 strips bacon
  • 3/4 cup cheese
  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Put 1 cup of water in the bottom of your Instant Pot and your trivet
  2. Cook the bacon, then crumble and distribute into mason jars
  3. Add eggs, cheese, cottage cheese, cream, and salt to blender, blend until smooth
  4. Divvy egg mixture into mason jars
  5. Cover each jar with foil, place in Instant Pot
  6. Set Instant Pot to "Steam" for 8 minutes
  7. Use Natural Pressure Release for about 10 minutes, then you can Quick Release the pressure
  8. Remove egg bites and let cool
